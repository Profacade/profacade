About Profacade

Profacade Exterior Solutions Inc. specializes in the professional installation of window wall, curtain wall, and glass canopy systems. We focus on reliable field execution, quality workmanship, and practical site coordination for high-rise residential and commercial construction projects.

Services

Window Wall

Professional installation of window wall systems for high-rise residential and mixed-use developments.

Curtain Wall

Field installation of curtain wall systems including site coordination, layout, sequencing, and exterior glazing support.

Glass Canopy

Installation of exterior glass canopy systems for entrances, podiums, and architectural façade features.
