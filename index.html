About Profacade

Profacade Exterior Solutions Inc. specializes in the professional installation of window wall, curtain wall, and glass canopy systems for high-rise residential and commercial construction projects.

We focus on dependable field execution, disciplined site coordination, and quality-driven installation. Our approach is practical, organized, and built around the realities of active construction sites, helping projects move forward with clear communication and reliable performance.

What We Deliver

  • Reliable installation of façade systems for high-rise projects
  • Practical coordination with site teams and project stakeholders
  • Strong attention to workmanship, sequencing, and execution
  • Professional support for bidding, planning, and installation delivery

Services

We provide installation services tailored to the needs of complex exterior envelope and façade scopes.

01

Window Wall

Professional installation of window wall systems for high-rise residential and mixed-use developments, with careful attention to sequencing, access, and site conditions.

02

Curtain Wall

Field installation of curtain wall systems including layout, coordination, installation sequencing, and exterior glazing support for demanding project environments.

03

Glass Canopy

Installation of exterior glass canopy systems for entrances, podiums, and architectural façade features, executed with a practical focus on safety and detailing.
