About Profacade

Profacade Exterior Solutions Inc. is an installation-focused façade partner specializing in window wall, curtain wall, glass canopy, and related exterior systems for high-rise residential and commercial projects.

Our role is simple and valuable: when the project team already has the system, supplier, or product source in place, Profacade delivers the field installation. We support projects that require a dependable installation contractor capable of coordinating site realities, sequencing work properly, and executing with consistency and professionalism.

Why Clients Work With Us

  • Installation support for both locally sourced and imported façade systems
  • Strong fit for general installation packages, supplier-led projects, and owner-supply procurement structures
  • Practical coordination with general contractors, manufacturers, and site teams
  • Reliable field execution for high-rise and technically demanding installations
